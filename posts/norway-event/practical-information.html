How to get to Leikanger

    1. Flight via Oslo (Oslo Airport, code OSL) to Sogndal (Sogndal Airport, code SOG).
      Flights to and from Sogndal is operated by Widerøe, which has several departures a day. Flight time is approximately one hour.
    2. Taxi from the airport to Leikanger (approx. 45 minutes).
    1. Flight to Bergen (Bergen Airport which is an international airport, code BGO).
    2. Stay the weekend in Bergen (to visit the beautiful city of Bergen, the second largest city of Norway).
      • Expressboat from Bergen to Leikanger along the beautiful Sognefjord (the largest and the deepest fjord in Norway, see e.g. this time lapse movie of a similar trip with this Expressboat). According to the current schedule, the Expressboat departs from the city center of Bergen 16:30 and arrives at Leikanger 20:45.
      • Flight from Bergen to Leikanger, operated by Widerøe.

How to get from Leikanger

  • Most probably one has to stop over in Oslo since the meeting will take a whole day.
    According to the current schedule, the probably most suitable flight from Sogndal to Oslo departs 18:10 and arrives at the Oslo Airport 19:00 (the next direct flight departs 21:00). The Airport Express train takes approx. 20 minutes to the city center of Oslo.
  • Expressboat along the beautiful Sognefjord (the largest and the deepest fjord in Norway, see e.g. this timelapse movie of a similar trip with this Expressboat) from Leikanger to Flåm, then train along the famous Flåm Line (famous because of its steep gradient and picturesque nature) to Myrdal, there from train to either Oslo or Bergen.
