Hybrid Event on

The Role of Semantic Technologies
in Common European Data Spaces

June 9th, Athens, Greece

Join us for the upcoming hybrid event titled "The Role of Semantic Technologies in Common European Data Spaces", co-organized by the National Technical University of Athens and Datoptron, on June 9th in Athens, Greece. The event promises an enriching program with presentations from esteemed experts representing SEMIC, Interoperable Europe Academy & Joinup, Data Space Support Center, DG CNECT, Data Space for Cultural Heritage, STIRData, University of Ghent, European Railway Association, and Cognizone.
